Hello!

My name is Baron von Messerschmidt, and I consider myself a rather interesting person. That is why my friends actually often me Mr. Fantastic or The Fantastic Baron.
I was born in Munich in 1978 but have since moved to Great Britain, where I found myself a charming castle. I have also lived briefly in Dubai, Mexico and Antarctica, but those places never really appreciated my fantastic personality and talents.
Football is just one of my many hobbies, as I also love to paint, swim and cook original medieval cuisine. I used to love sword fighting and pole jumping but a number of unfortunate accidents have forced me to abandon those wonderful sports. It is a shame, though, because I am extremely good at everything I do, and I was frankly close to setting quite a few world records.
